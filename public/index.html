How it works

01

Upload your image

Drag & drop or browse. JPEG, PNG, and WebP up to 20MB. Your file goes to the Node.js server's memory — never stored permanently.

02

Sharp processes it

The backend uses Sharp — a high-performance C++ image library. It reencodes the image at your chosen quality, format, and dimensions using libvips.

03

Download, compare, repeat

The before/after slider reveals the visual difference. Tweak quality, recompress, until you hit the size-quality tradeoff you want.
